Creamy Mashed Potatoes Recipe

Description

These creamy, cheesy mashed potatoes taste great without gravy or garlic, and make a great side dish for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 large potatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 slice American cheese

Steps

  1. Skin the potatoes with a peeler.
  2. Chop the potatoes into smaller pieces.
  3. Cover the potatoes with water.
  4. Bring pot to a boil and cook until potatoes fall apart.
  5. Dump most of the potato water.
  6. Add heavy cream, butter, and cheese to pot over low heat.
  7. Mash potatoes, adding additional cream and butter to taste.
