Roasted Asparagus Recipe

Description

Asparagus tastes slighty nutty and sweet when roasted in the oven, and makes an excellent veggie side for steaks, chops, and fillets.

Ingredients

  • 1 bushel of asparagus
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1½ tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • ½ tsp groud black pepper
  • 1 lemon wedge

Steps

  1. Preheat the overn to 425℉.
  2. Trim off the woody ends of the aspargus, about 1 inch.
  3. Add aspragus into baking dish and coat with olive oil, cheese, salt, garlic powder.
  4. Roast for 12-15 minutes, when stalks are easily piered with a fork.
  5. Spritz with lemon juice to taste.
